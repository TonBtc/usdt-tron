About the Project

Tether USD Test Stablecoin is designed for developers and researchers to test decentralized finance (DeFi) applications on the TRON blockchain. It aims to simulate a USD-pegged stablecoin for educational and experimental purposes, offering low-cost transactions and high scalability.

Our team consists of blockchain enthusiasts dedicated to advancing DeFi innovation on TRON. This token is not intended for real-world financial transactions but serves as a tool for learning and development.
